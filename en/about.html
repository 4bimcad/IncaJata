Who We Are

INCAJATA S.A.C. (RUC: 20614558491) is a consulting and technical company that provides comprehensive solutions for the mining and productive sectors. We specialize in document automation, compliance management, technical project development, and operational support for extractive and industrial companies.

We combine multidisciplinary experience, applied technology, and an approach focused on efficiency, sustainability, and measurable results. Our team works with modern project management methodologies, ensuring that each initiative meets national and international standards.

We offer services ranging from strategic planning, exploration, and technical analysis to mineral processing and operational optimization. In addition, we support investment and financing efforts for mining projects, facilitating strategic partnerships and growth opportunities.

At INCAJATA, innovation, safety, and social responsibility are fundamental pillars of our operations, ensuring reliable, scalable projects with a positive impact on communities and the environment.
